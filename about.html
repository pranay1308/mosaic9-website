About Us

Built by someone who's already scaled and sold one of these.

Mosaic9 exists because most brands don't need another vendor. They need one accountable partner who has actually built a company before, not just pitched for one.

MISSION

Why we exist

To give ambitious brands a single, senior-led marketing partner — one that combines strategy, creative, performance and technology into one connected system instead of nine disconnected vendors, and that is judged on revenue and growth, not impressions.

VISION

Where we're headed

To become India's benchmark for full-service, data-and-AI-backed marketing — the ecosystem growing companies point to when they explain what "accountable partner" is supposed to mean.

What we believe

The principles behind every brief

01

One partner, zero gaps

Strategy, creative, performance and tech report into a single account team. No misaligned agencies, no briefing gaps, one partner who owns outcomes end-to-end.

02

Every brief starts with a business goal

Not a deliverable. We measure success in revenue, leads and growth — not just reach and impressions.

03

Senior-led, always

Senior strategists are engaged from day one through execution. You never get handed to a junior-only team after the pitch is won.

04

Transparent, on principle

Live dashboards, monthly reviews and proactive communication. You always know what we're doing, what it's delivering, and what's next.
